CUAD dataset

Contract Understanding Atticus Dataset

Ingest 510 expert-annotated commercial contracts from the Atticus Project's CUAD dataset (CC BY 4.0). Covers 41 clause types including change of control, IP ownership, non-compete, indemnification, and more — sourced from real SEC filings.

Ingest CUAD contracts

Download annotated contracts from HuggingFace and ingest them into the corpus. Each contract includes CUAD clause annotations identifying 41 key legal provisions.
